- Request this **in writing** (email is fine)

## If No Response After Multiple Attempts
- Document all your outreach attempts with dates
- After 60-90 days of no response, you've demonstrated good faith
- Consider sending a final "certified letter" for paper trail
- Consult with a lawyer if the value warrants it ($600-900 range)
